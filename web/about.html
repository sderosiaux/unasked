Why I built this

I'm in meetings all day. Important decisions get made, action items get assigned, but by the end of the week, half of it is forgotten or buried in notes nobody reads.

I needed something different: a real-time collaborator that doesn't just capture—it questions. When an assumption slips by unchallenged, I want it flagged. When a question hangs in the air unanswered, I want it surfaced. Not for my notes—for the conversation itself.

Unasked is that AI collaborator. It listens, extracts what matters, and surfaces the tensions before they become problems. Less time in follow-up meetings. More time actually getting things done.

This tool is built for people who lead conversations and need to stay focused on the discussion—not on taking notes. Let the AI handle the details while you drive the meeting forward.
