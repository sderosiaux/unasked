Questions & Answers

Is my meeting data private?

Yes. Audio is processed in real-time and never stored. Your conversations go directly to the transcription and AI services, then disappear. Nothing is saved on any server.

Do I need to create an account?

No account needed. Just download the app, add your API keys in Settings, and start your first meeting. It takes less than 2 minutes to get started.

What languages are supported?

Unasked currently supports English and French. The AI automatically detects which language is being spoken during your meeting.

How much does it cost?

The app is free. You bring your own API keys from Anthropic (for AI analysis) and Deepgram (for transcription). Both offer generous free tiers to get started.

Can I use it for virtual meetings?

Yes. Unasked captures audio from your microphone, so it works with any meeting platformâ€”Zoom, Teams, Google Meet, or in-person discussions.

App blocked by macOS?

Since Unasked isn't notarized by Apple yet, macOS Gatekeeper may block it. This is normal for open-source apps distributed outside the App Store.

To fix this:

  1. Open Terminal (search for it in Spotlight)
  2. Copy and paste this command:
xattr -cr "/Applications/Unasked.app"
  1. Press Enter and try opening the app again

This removes the quarantine flag that macOS adds to downloaded files.
