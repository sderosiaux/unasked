Why I built this

I'm in meetings all day. Important decisions get made, action items get assigned, but by the end of the week, half of it is forgotten or buried in notes nobody reads.

I needed something different: a real-time collaborator that could catch what I miss. When we're going in circles on a topic, I want to know immediately—not three meetings later. When someone makes an assumption, I want it flagged so we can dig deeper right now, not schedule another call to clarify.

Meeting Copilot is that AI collaborator. It listens, extracts what matters, and surfaces the tensions before they become problems. Less time in follow-up meetings. More time actually getting things done.

Questions & Answers

Is my meeting data private?

Yes. Audio is processed in real-time and never stored. Your conversations go directly to the transcription and AI services, then disappear. Nothing is saved on any server.

Do I need to create an account?

No account needed. Just download the app, add your API keys in Settings, and start your first meeting. It takes less than 2 minutes to get started.

What languages are supported?

Meeting Copilot currently supports English and French. The AI automatically detects which language is being spoken during your meeting.

How much does it cost?

The app is free. You bring your own API keys from Anthropic (for AI analysis) and Deepgram (for transcription). Both offer generous free tiers to get started.

Can I use it for virtual meetings?

Yes. Meeting Copilot captures audio from your microphone, so it works with any meeting platform—Zoom, Teams, Google Meet, or in-person discussions.
